The Process

Seven steps from question to clarity.

01
Free Consultation Call
A 15-minute call with Gordon. Tell him what's going on — whether you've been waiting months for a specific scan, need a test you can't afford at home, or want a comprehensive health screen you've been putting off. No forms, no commitment. Just a conversation about your situation.
02
Your Tailored Plan
Based on your consultation and needs, Gordon researches the best hospital and program suitable for you, and comes back with 2–3 tailored options covering cost, timeline, and everything you need to make an informed decision. You choose what fits.
03
Booking
Once you're aligned on a plan, Gordon coordinates your appointment with the hospital's international department. Dates confirmed, everything arranged, nothing left to figure out on your end.
04
Pre-Trip Preparation
Your personalised preparation guide arrives 2–3 weeks before travel. VPN setup. Alipay guidance. Dietary and fasting instructions. What to bring and what to expect. An emergency contact sheet with everything you need. A check-in call happens 48 hours before you fly.
05
Your Visit
Arrive at the hospital. The international department's coordinator accompanies you throughout. Tests completed — typically in a single morning or full day. Payment made directly to the hospital on the day at Chinese public rates.
06
Your Results Arrive
Results are processed after your visit. For most clients this takes 10–15 days. Gordon follows up as soon as your results are ready, confirms receipt, and walks through next steps with you.
07
Back Home With a Plan
Gordon debriefs with you and helps you bring your results to your doctor effectively — what to ask for, how to frame the findings, what follow-up to request. Your trip becomes action, not just information.
