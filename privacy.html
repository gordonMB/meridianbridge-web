Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Meridian Bridge ("we," "us," or "our") is a marketing and client services company registered in British Columbia, Canada. This policy explains what personal information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. We are committed to handling your information responsibly and in accordance with Canada's Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA), British Columbia's Personal Information Protection Act (PIPA), and, where applicable to visitors in the United Kingdom, the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR).

What we collect

We only collect information you choose to provide to us, which may include:

  • Your name and email address, when you submit an inquiry form or book a consultation
  • Your country of residence and the nature of your inquiry
  • Any information you share with us during a consultation call or by email, which may include details about your health goals or medical needs
  • Basic technical information your browser provides automatically, such as general location and the pages you visit, through standard website analytics

How we use your information

We use the information you provide to:

  • Respond to your inquiry and arrange your consultation
  • Recommend suitable hospitals and packages for your situation
  • Coordinate bookings with partner hospitals on your behalf
  • Send you relevant follow-up communications and information about our services
  • Improve our website and the quality of our service

We will only send you marketing communications where you have contacted us or shown interest in our services, and you can opt out at any time using the unsubscribe link in any email.

Sensitive and health-related information

Because of the nature of our service, you may choose to share health-related information with us. We treat this information with particular care. We only use it to help coordinate your health check and recommend appropriate options, and we only share it with a partner hospital with your knowledge in order to arrange your booking. We are not a medical provider and do not create or hold formal medical records.

Who we share it with

We do not sell your personal information. We share it only in these limited circumstances:

  • With partner hospitals, where necessary to coordinate your booking and only with your knowledge
  • With trusted service providers that help us operate, such as our email, scheduling, and form tools — who process information on our behalf and are bound to protect it
  • Where required by law

International transfers

Because our service involves coordinating healthcare in China, some information you provide may be shared with partner hospitals located in the People's Republic of China in order to arrange your booking. We share only what is necessary for that purpose, and only with your knowledge.

Your rights

You have the right to:

  • Ask what personal information we hold about you
  • Request that we correct or update your information
  • Request that we delete your information
  • Withdraw your consent and opt out of communications at any time

To exercise any of these rights, email us at gordon@meridianbridge.ca and we will respond promptly.

How we protect your information

We take reasonable measures to protect your information from loss, misuse, and unauthorised access. We retain your information only for as long as necessary to provide our service and meet our legal obligations.

Cookies and analytics

Our website may use basic cookies and analytics to understand how visitors use the site. You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time without affecting your ability to use the site.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "last updated" date at the top of this page.

Contact us

If you have any questions about this policy or how we handle your information, please contact us at gordon@meridianbridge.ca.
